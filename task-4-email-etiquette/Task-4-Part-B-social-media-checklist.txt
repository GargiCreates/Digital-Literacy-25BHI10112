Social Media Do’s and Don’ts :
 	Do’s:
•	Think before posting – Consider how your content may be perceived in the future. 
•	Maintain professionalism – Keep your profile respectful and appropriate. 
•	Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and limit personal information sharing. 
•	Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading misinformation. 
•	Use social media for learning and networking – Follow educational pages and connect with professionals. 

 	Don’ts:
•	Don’t overshare personal details – Avoid posting sensitive information like address or phone number. 
•	Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content – It can harm your reputation. 
•	Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – Maintain respectful communication. 
•	Don’t trust unknown links or messages – They may be phishing or scams. 
•	Don’t ignore your digital footprint – Everything you post can have long-term consequences.
